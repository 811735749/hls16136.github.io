My name is Hannah, but online I'm known as PaintMePastel. I've been doing digital art for a year now, creating many various types of art to sell and as gifts for friends and family. My main goal with my work is to improve my art and continue growing as an artist. This website is designed to be a portfolio for my digital art and be useful for commission information for people that are interested in buying my artwork.

A bit of information about me and my journey with art: I had always been interested in drawing on paper since I was little, probably around 5 or 6 years old. I've done many hyper-realistic pieces with pencil and paper, acrylic paints, in general just many, many pieces with physical objects. As of 2 years ago when I was around 16-17, I got invested in digital art as a means to produce creative content online. This industry is blooming all throughout social media and entertainment platforms, and I've found myself a part of these industries mostly by accident. I stream video games on Twitch and made my own graphics for my stream. People asked about my art and wanted to buy some of my work so they could use it, and I've been rolling with that for about a year now. My style is pretty new and I'm still figuring things out as I go, but I've improved quite a bit over this past year!
